code/game/gamemodes/changeling/changeling_powers.dm

/datum/power/changelingThe changeling subtype of /datum/power. Contains a lot of custom logic for chemicals, activation checks, and so on.
/datum/power/changeling/evolution_menuThe power that changelings use to unlock new abilities. Has no effects on its own.
/datum/power/changeling/absorb_dnaOne of the three core changeling powers. After holding someone in a kill grab and channeling for long enough, kills them and absorbs their DNA.
/datum/power/changeling/transformOne of the three core changeling abilities. Transforms the changeling into a simulacrum of a genome they've absorbed.
/datum/power/changeling/regenerative_stasisOne of the three core changeling abilities. Enters a fake death state that can end with a full regeneration, up to and including actual death.
/datum/power/changeling/hive_uploadAllows the changeling to share an absorbed_dna datum with other changelings by putting it into a shared list.
/datum/power/changeling/hive_downloadUsed to "download" DNA from the shared bank that Hive Channel adds to.
/datum/power/changeling/stingDummy changeling sting type. Has no effects on its own, but contains all the logic required for a sting.
/datum/power/changeling/sting/deaf_stingCauses deafness in a target for 15 ticks.
/datum/power/changeling/sting/blind_stingCauses blindness in a target for 10 ticks, blurriness for 20 ticks, and nearsightedness for 30 ticks.
/datum/power/changeling/sting/silence_stingSilences a target for 30 seconds.
/datum/power/changeling/sting/extract_dnaExtracts the DNA from a target, giving the changeling their DNA as a disguise without gaining any genome points.
/datum/power/changeling/sting/hallucination_stingCauses heavy hallucinations after a short delay.
/datum/power/changeling/sting/death_stingInjects someone with a massive dose of lexorin.
/datum/power/changeling/mimic_voiceAllows the changeling to mimic any voice they choose. Very fun!
/datum/power/changeling/lesser_formTurns the changeling into a monkey. They're able to revert back after a short period.
/datum/power/changeling/boost_rangeBoosts the range of the next sting by one tile.
/datum/power/changeling/epinephrine_sacsImmediately removes all stuns and wakes us up if we're unconscious.
/datum/power/changeling/rapid_chemical_synthesisPassive, doubles natural chem regen. At time of writing, this means it goes from 0.5 to 1.
/datum/power/changeling/engorged_chemical_glandsPassive, boosts chem storage by 25 points.
/datum/power/changeling/digital_camouflageMakes you untrackable by AI, but gives you unique examine text. Free because the AI currently doesn't exist, making this primarily for RPing.
/datum/power/changeling/rapid_regenerationHeals 10 of brute/fire/tox/oxy damage per second for 10 seconds. Can't be "restarted" while it's already active. (unintentional?)
